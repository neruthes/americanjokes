Sam the farmer got a fish from the river. He went home and told his wife, "we have fish & fries for dinner!"

"No oil."

"Then boil."

"No pot."

"Then bake."

"No lighter."

Sam threw the fish back. The fish rolled up and raised his right fin, shouted, "Make America Great Again!"
