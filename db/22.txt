A Chinese worker sent his boss a request that he wanted to leave for China.

"Why, my friend? Why do you want to leave the land of freedom?"

"Oh, I got a primary reason and a secondary reason. The secondary one is that the epidemic in USA is still in control but I doubt it will not last long."

"No worry, boy. There will never be a Covid-19 outbreak here."

"Alright, this is my primary reason."
